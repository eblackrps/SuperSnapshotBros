# Changelog

All notable project milestones are tracked here.

## v1.3.0 - World 1 Finale and Water Front

- Added `World 1-6`, `1-7`, and `1-8`, completing World 1 as an eight-stage arc.
- Added the `Split-Brain Sovereign` castle boss at the end of `1-8`.
- Added `World 2-1 Tidal Mirror` with a water-world theme, `Tide Skimmer` enemies, and undertow hazards.
- Added theme-aware rendering for datacenter, fortress, and water stages.
- Polished the new stages with theme-specific ambience, lighter water-world physics, and updated campaign-complete messaging.
- Cleaned up the repository with a standalone `LICENSE`, updated `README`, and this changelog.

## v1.2.1 - Production Final Polish

- Hardened respawns and transient-state resets.
- Cleaned up item placement, readability issues, and brick routes in `1-1`.
- Finalized the initial five-stage production build.

## v1.2.0 - Failover Spine and Snapshot Bastion

- Added `World 1-4` and `World 1-5`.
- Added the `Rollback Snapshot` powerup and the `Ransomware Warden` mini-boss.
- Expanded the HUD with life/redundancy display and boss health.

## v1.1.0 - World 1-3 Immutable Backup

- Built out progression through `World 1-3`.
- Added corruption fields and the `Immutable Backup` mechanic.

## v1.0.0 - Hypervisor Crash

- Initial public release of `World 1-1`.
