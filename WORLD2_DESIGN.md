# World 2 Design Pack

## Theme

World 2 shifts from neon datacenter shelves into flooded recovery infrastructure.
The tone is cooler, slower, and more fluid: submerged lanes, buoy relays, undertow
pressure, vent lifts, and waterlogged mirror routes.

## Mechanical Identity

- `Undertow` pulls the player sideways across unsafe lanes.
- `Vent Lift` creates vertical traversal pockets and recovery shortcuts.
- Water-theme physics soften gravity and reduce max fall speed.
- `Tide Skimmers` sweep floating routes and pressure the player off stable footing.
- Buoy lifts replace the clean server-rail feel of World 1 sync platforms.

## Current Stages

- `2-1 Tidal Mirror`
  Introduces undertow lanes, buoy lifts, and the world shift.
- `2-2 Flooded Replica`
  Adds vent lifts and mirror-lock traversal.
- `2-3 Undertow Exchange`
  Pushes harder current routing and vent-fed recovery lines.

## Next Targets

- `2-4 Breakwater Failover`
  Dense routing through alternating undertow directions and timed lifts.
- `2-5 Subsea Vault`
  A deeper blue finale stage with a stronger DR payoff and the first World 2 boss lead-in.

## Visual Direction

- Cyan, sea-glass, and pale gold accent colors
- Bubble ambience and submerged lighting instead of rack LEDs
- More open vertical airspace and fewer hard fortress silhouettes
